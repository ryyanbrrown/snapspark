Returns Policy

At SnapSpark we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We accept returns under the following conditions:

  • Product is unused: Returns are accepted for products that have not been used, are in their original packaging, and are returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • Product is defective: If your product is defective or damaged upon receipt, we will provide a full refund upon return of the item.
  • Other Reasons: If you have another reason for returning the product, please contact us to discuss your request. We’ll work with you to ensure a fair resolution.

To initiate a return, please contact us using the contact form or email us at snapsparkcoe@outlook.com with your order details and reason for return. We’ll provide instructions for returning the product.
